How accurate is CloudVibes weather forecast?

CloudVibes provides highly accurate weather forecasts using data from Open-Meteo API, which aggregates data from multiple meteorological services worldwide. Our forecasts are updated daily and include real-time temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation data.

Is CloudVibes weather forecast free?

Yes! CloudVibes is completely free to use. You can check weather forecasts for any city worldwide, view 7-day predictions, and access real-time weather data without any subscription or payment.

How often is weather data updated?

Weather data on CloudVibes is updated automatically every day at 6 AM. The page also auto-refreshes every hour to ensure you always have the most current weather information.

Can I check weather for any city in the world?

Yes! CloudVibes supports weather forecasts for thousands of cities worldwide. Simply use the search bar to enter any city name and get instant weather information including current conditions and 7-day forecast.

What weather information does CloudVibes provide?

CloudVibes provides comprehensive weather information including: current temperature, feels-like temperature, humidity levels, cloud cover percentage, wind speed, precipitation amounts, 7-day forecast with min/max temperatures, and an interactive temperature trend chart.
